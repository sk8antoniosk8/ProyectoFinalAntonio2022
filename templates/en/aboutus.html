Spanish Academy "El Faro"

Our Spanish language school located in Málaga is one of the top 5 award-winning schools in our city. It has spacious premises, well located geographically, which can be reached by EMT bus lines 22, 34, 12 and 5, and the metro is less than 10 minutes walk away. Our academy consists of 3 spacious classrooms. In each of them we teach a different level of Spanish (A2, B1 y B2) and all of them have the necessary resources and teaching material to carry out the lessons.

We have a team of native teachers with a great deal of experience in teaching Spanish as a foreign language (ELE). They give classes entirely in Spanish, adapting to the level and needs of the students.

We have a 90% pass rate among our students. So as soon as you register with us you are almost guaranteed a pass, without forgetting the most important thing, which is to learn one of the most spoken languages in the world today according to the Instituto Cervantes.
